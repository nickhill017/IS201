Pronouns

  1. ich- I
  2. You has different variations depending on context
    • du- you (informal)
    • ihr- you (informal towards a group of people)
    • Sie- you (Formal)

      In German there is a form of formality between younger generation and older generation. This is where the formal speech is needed.

  3. er/sie/es- he/she/it
  4. wir- we
